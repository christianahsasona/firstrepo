Preparation of yam pottage.

Ingredient Steps Required
yam,crayfish,pepper(grinded or fresh),onions,palm oil,smoked fish,seasoning pill off the bark of the yam and slice into small pieces. rinse
thouroughly and arrange in a clean pot. Add water to cover the yam and some
pinch of salt; and their heat to cook(soften). Blend your fresh pepper and onions
and set aside. Rinse the Smoked fish and crayfish and De-bone it.When the yam is soften,(make sure the water in it is not much)
pour in the blended pepper, add the fish to it (smoked and crayfish),add palm oil and seasoned to taste and then stir
allow it to heat for about 7 to 10 minutes and then you can mash the yam if you love it mashed; and if you don't; your yam pottage is ready.

In the aspect of this tutorial i will be explaining how to prepare egusi soup
using thr frying method.

Ingrdients Steps Required
melon seed,palm oil,cow skin(ponmo),onion pepper,
crayfish,locust bean(iru),pumpkin leaves,beef, seasoning		 make sure you wash the leaves, ponmo,and iru properly and clean.wash and cook the beef properly.(sieve the beef from the stock)
blend your melon seed with little amount of water.Blend the pepper, onion, crayfish(not compulsory to blend) and iru.
add little amount od the blended Ingredient to the egusi and mix . Put a clean pot on heat
and then add palmoil(a full cooking spoon measurement) and allow to heat a little, put the mixed egusi and allow to fry for about 5 to 7 minutes
add the blended pepper, crayfish, the stock(water from the beef.),ponmo,leavesand seasoned to taste. leave on medium heat for about 10minutes and
egusi soup is ready!!!. You can serve with pounded yam, Amala or Tuwo.
